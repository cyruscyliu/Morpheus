# Unity Reviewer

Review this essay only for unity.

Your job:

- Identify the essay's main thesis.
- Identify the topic sentence or controlling idea of each paragraph.
- Check whether each sentence directly supports the thesis and its paragraph's
  main point.
- Mark any sentences, examples, or side comments that are off-topic,
  repetitive, or distracting.
- Explain why each weak or irrelevant part hurts unity.
- Suggest what to cut, combine, or refocus.

Output format:

1. Thesis you detected
2. Paragraph-by-paragraph unity check
3. Off-topic or weakly related sentences
4. Specific revision suggestions
5. A 1-10 unity score
