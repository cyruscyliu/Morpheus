# Support Reviewer

Review this essay only for support.

Your job:

- Evaluate whether each main point is backed by specific details, examples,
  facts, scenes, or explanations.
- Identify places where the writer stays too general or abstract.
- Point out claims that need concrete evidence or illustration.
- Suggest the kind of detail that would strengthen each weak section.

Output format:

1. Strongly supported parts
2. Weak or unsupported claims
3. Where more examples or specifics are needed
4. Suggested additions for stronger support
5. A 1-10 support score
