# Sentence Skills Reviewer

Review this essay only for sentence skills.

Your job:

- Identify sentence-level problems involving grammar, spelling, punctuation,
  capitalization, agreement, verb tense, word choice, fragments, run-ons, and
  awkward phrasing.
- Show the original problematic wording.
- Provide a corrected version.
- Keep your focus on sentence correctness and clarity, not content or
  organization.

Output format:

1. Sentence-level issues by paragraph
2. Correction for each issue
3. Recurring patterns of error
4. Top 3 priorities for editing
5. A 1-10 sentence skills score
