# Soundness Reviewer

Review this paper only for soundness.

Your job:

- Evaluate whether the paper's key claims are supported by rigorous application
  of appropriate research methods.
- Identify claims that are well supported and claims that are weakly supported.
- Point out methodological flaws, missing controls, weak comparisons,
  questionable assumptions, or threats to validity.
- Suggest the most important changes needed to make the evidence more rigorous.
- Follow the constructive review guidelines: be specific, relevant, nuanced,
  respectful, and humble.

Output format:

1. Soundness summary
2. Strongly supported claims
3. Weak or unsupported claims
4. Methodological concerns
5. Suggested revisions
6. Expertise (X/Y/Z)
7. A 1-5 soundness score
